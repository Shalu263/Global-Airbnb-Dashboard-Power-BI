## Airbnb Analytics Dashboard
####  Project Overview:
##### This project analyzes Airbnb data to uncover insights related to customer behavior, review patterns, and seasonal trends. The dashboard is built using Power BI and focuses on delivering actionable business insights.

####  Objectives:
##### Understand reviewer behavior and engagement
##### Identify seasonal trends across major cities
##### Analyze trust and verification signals
##### Build an interactive and visually compelling dashboard

#### Key Insights:
##### Majority of users contribute only one review, indicating low repeat engagement
##### Paris and Rome dominate during summer (April–August)
##### New York peaks during holiday season (Nov–Dec)
##### Most hosts are verified, increasing platform trust

#### Dashboard Features:
##### Review Frequency Analysis (Pareto Chart)
##### Market Share by City
##### Seasonal Trends Visualization
##### Trust & Verification Insights

#### Tools Used:
##### Power BI
##### DAX
##### Data Cleaning & Transformation
